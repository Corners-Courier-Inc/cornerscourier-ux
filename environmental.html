Your Trusted Wastewater & Environmental Courier

Corners Courier provides reliable, chain-of-custody–compliant transport for wastewater samples, drinking water samples, soil and sediment samples, and other environmental materials requiring precise handling and timely delivery to certified laboratories.

We understand how critical sample integrity is for regulatory reporting, wastewater treatment operations, engineering assessments, EPD/EPA requirements, and municipal compliance. Every delivery is handled with documented, secure, timestamped chain-of-custody from pickup to lab intake.

  • Wastewater grab samples (influent/effluent, NPDES, EPD/EPA compliance)
  • Drinking water samples for potability & contamination testing
  • Soil & sediment samples for environmental and engineering analysis
  • Stormwater & groundwater media
  • Industrial discharge & manufacturing facility samples

We serve environmental labs, municipalities, engineering firms, manufacturers, and water treatment facilities throughout Georgia, Tennessee, South Carolina, North Carolina, Alabama, and Florida.

Chain-of-Custody & Regulatory Compliance

Our drivers follow strict environmental sample handling guidelines, maintain proper temperature control, and complete all required chain-of-custody documentation for lab intake. Each handoff is timestamped and recorded to ensure accuracy for regulatory testing and engineering reports. We proudly serve environmental clients across Georgia, Tennessee, South Carolina, North Carolina, Alabama, and Jacksonville, FL.

  • Timestamped handoffs & signed COC forms
  • Cooler transport & cold pack temperature maintenance
  • Lab-specific packaging & labeling compliance
  • 24/7 pickup availability for urgent wastewater samples
