Georgia Courier FAQs

We have a film production shooting outside of Atlanta. Can you deliver equipment same-day?

Yes. We provide urgent, same-day courier services for the film industry, delivering props, equipment, and documents directly to sets anywhere in Georgia.

Our couriers are experienced in the proper handling and transport of environmental samples, including chain-of-custody procedures required for GA EPD (Georgia Environmental Protection Division) compliance.

Absolutely. We can create customized, scheduled routes to support your logistics and supply chain needs, including regular transfers between Savannah and Atlanta.

For rush service in metro Atlanta, we can typically dispatch a driver for pickup within an hour, ensuring your package is on its way as quickly as possible.
