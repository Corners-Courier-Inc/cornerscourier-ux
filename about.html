ABOUT CORNERS COURIER INC

A trusted, specialized courier for environmental and medical logistics across the Southeast

Our Mission

Corners Courier Inc was created with one clear purpose: to deliver critical environmental and medical samples safely, securely, and on time — every time. These materials are highly sensitive and often time-critical. Whether it’s a wastewater test, a clinical specimen, or a research sample, the accuracy of its results depends on how it is transported. We take that responsibility seriously.

Our Core Values

  • Reliability: 24/7 availability for urgent and scheduled deliveries.
  • Security: Documented chain of custody for sensitive materials.
  • Professionalism: Courteous, trained, insured drivers.
  • Precision: Every delivery handled with accuracy and attention to detail.
  • Integrity: Clear communication, transparent pricing, and ethical operations.

What We Deliver

  • Environmental samples: wastewater, surface water, soil, sediment, and air media
  • Medical logistics: clinical specimens, lab work, biomedical samples, pharmaceutical items
  • Business parcel delivery: documents, parts, and time-sensitive packages
  • Full coverage across our service areas

Commitment to Compliance

We follow industry-standard procedures for handling and securing sensitive environmental and medical materials. Whether a delivery supports public health, environmental monitoring, or clinical diagnostics, we treat each one with the highest level of care.
