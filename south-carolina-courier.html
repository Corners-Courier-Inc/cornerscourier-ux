South Carolina Courier FAQs

My company is part of the Greenville-Spartanburg automotive corridor. Can you handle urgent parts delivery?

Yes. We specialize in on-demand and scheduled deliveries for industrial and manufacturing clients in the Upstate region, ensuring your supply chain keeps moving.

Do you handle sensitive environmental samples from coastal areas near Charleston?

Absolutely. We are experienced in transporting sensitive coastal and marine samples, ensuring they are handled with proper chain-of-custody and temperature control to labs in Charleston or Columbia.

We need to send legal documents from Columbia to a client in Hilton Head. How fast can you do it?

We can pick up and deliver same-day. For maximum speed, our "direct drive" service means the driver goes straight to the destination with only your package on board.

Are you compliant with SCDHEC requirements for water sample transport?

Yes, our drivers are trained in handling environmental samples according to SCDHEC guidelines, including proper documentation and temperature-aware transport.
