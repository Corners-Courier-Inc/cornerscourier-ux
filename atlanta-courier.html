Atlanta Courier FAQs

How quickly can you deliver a package from Midtown to the Airport (ATL)?

With our rush service, we can often make the delivery in under 90 minutes, depending heavily on I-75/I-85 traffic. We know the best routes and times to avoid peak congestion.

Yes. We specialize in time-critical legal deliveries. Inform us of your deadline, and we will dispatch a dedicated driver to ensure your filings are submitted on time.

Do you offer courier services for the film industry on weekends?

Absolutely. We operate 24/7, including weekends and holidays, to support Atlanta's dynamic film and television production schedules.

Our lab is near the CDC. Do you provide scheduled routes for medical specimen pickup?

Yes, we provide reliable, scheduled courier routes for many healthcare and research institutions in the Emory/CDC corridor and throughout Metro Atlanta.
